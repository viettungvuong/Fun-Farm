### About game:  
In a village, a farmer was living peacefully with his farm and house. One day, he discovered his land was under siege by hordes of mischievous slimes who want to destroy your farm and relentless skeletons who have you on their Most Wanted list. To protect his home, the farmer armed himself with weapons and decided to fight back against the enemies. And you have to take care of yourself too...
